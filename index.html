The drama unit of my school fellowship (Calvary Love Theatre) was to have a drama rehearsal that evening. I met Eunice already on the field- or was she the one who met me there? Can't remember the details now. But what stood out that day was that Eunice and I were the only people who came for that meeting. What was then supposed to be a drama rehearsal turned out to be the beginning of a talking stageü§£. I've been seeing her in the fellowship, UCHCF, before then, but that was the first time I would see her upclose. We spoke about Ogbomosho where her parents lived, I was really captured by her deep Yoruba accent sprinkled with Ogbomosho dialect- Kai! If you know, you know.

We also spoke about school activities, upcoming tests and the needed preparations. I initially had no intention, I was only trying to be a good senior colleague, telling her about past questions, ways to answer them, etc. But after we departed that day, I thought within my mind, this lady is not bad ooü•π. Adrenaline up, dopamine up, Testosterone up... I will stare at that picture for hours, analysing her smile, her lips, her teeth, everything just looked perfecto.

As if confirming my thoughts, a friend of mine entered my dm and said 'this lady has such a beautiful smile, is she our sister?' 'May be' I smiled in agreement.
